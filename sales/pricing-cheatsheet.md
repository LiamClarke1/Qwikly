## TOP-UPS

**R20 per extra qualified lead** over the monthly cap.

Starter caps at 25. Pro caps at 200. Premium has no cap.

If a Starter customer is hitting their 25 limit regularly, that's the natural moment to suggest Pro.

---

## ANNUAL PRICING

Pay annually and get **2 months free** (effectively 10 months for the price of 12).

| Plan | Monthly | Annual (per month) | Annual saving |
|---|---|---|---|
| Pro | R599 | R499 | R1,198/year |
| Premium | R1,299 | R1,082 | R2,598/year |

Offer this when a customer is confident and wants to lock in. Don't lead with it.

## RECOMMENDED PITCH BY BUSINESS TYPE

### Restaurant or cafe
**Pitch: Pro (R599/month)**
Restaurants need booking volume. 200 qualified leads a month fits the typical flow of table enquiries, function bookings, and catering requests. Custom greeting matters here, "Hi, looking to book a table or an event?" vs. a generic opener converts better. The lack of per-booking fees is a genuine differentiator vs. reservation platforms that take a cut.

Key hook: "Most restaurants miss dinner booking enquiries that come in between 2pm and 6pm when the kitchen is busy and the phone goes unanswered."

---

### Solo tradie (electrician, plumber, painter, roofer)
**Pitch: Start on Starter, upgrade when they see leads**
Solo tradies are sceptical by nature and cash-conscious. Let them start on Starter for free, get one or two real leads through, and then the upgrade conversation happens naturally. Most will hit the 25 cap within 3-4 weeks and ask to upgrade themselves.

Key hook: "You're already out on a job at 11pm and someone needs a quote. If they message your site, Qwikly picks it up. You get the details in the morning."

Don't oversell to tradies. Let the product do it.

---

### Salon or beauty studio
**Pitch: Pro (R599/month)**
Salons get a high volume of booking requests and are often too busy to answer the phone during appointments. Custom greeting and custom questions are valuable here, "What service are you looking to book? Do you have a preferred stylist?" Pro's custom qualifying questions make the leads that arrive much more useful than a generic "someone enquired."

Key hook: "Your receptionist is washing someone's hair. Qwikly answers the next caller's message automatically."

---

### Gym or fitness studio
**Pitch: Starter to try, Pro when they see conversion**
Gyms often have a membership coordinator or manager who handles enquiries. Walk them through Starter first, show them the leads coming in, and let them feel the volume before pitching Pro. Gyms with a high volume of class bookings will upgrade fast.

Key hook: "Every person who checks your website at 9pm and doesn't hear back is probably going to sign up at the gym around the corner."

---

### Clinic or medical practice
**Pitch: Pro (R599/month)**
Clinics have appointment-heavy workflows and are often understaffed at reception. Custom questions are essential here, "Is this a new patient or existing? What are you coming in for?" helps triage before the lead arrives. POPIA compliance is a natural selling point, mention it upfront.

Key hook: "Patients who can't book online after hours tend to call a different practice. Qwikly catches them before they do."

---

### Multi-location business or franchise
**Pitch: Premium (R1,299/month)**
Unlimited leads, API access, and the coming-soon features (WhatsApp routing, calendar integration) are what make Premium worthwhile for businesses with volume. If they have a marketing or operations person, talk to them, not just the owner.

Key hook: "You've got multiple locations and the enquiry volume to match. Premium means no cap and an API to plug Qwikly into whatever you're already running."

## DISCOUNT RULES

**Never give a discount in month one.** The product is already priced conservatively. Discounting before they've seen results devalues the product and creates a bad expectation for renewal.

**The only acceptable discount is the annual commit.** If they want to pay monthly, they pay the monthly rate. If they want to pay annually upfront, they get 2 months free. That's the deal.

**Founding 10 exception.** The first 10 customers get 50% off Pro for 6 months in exchange for a testimonial and permission to use their logo on the site. This is the only time a discount applies in month one. See [founding-customer-offer.md] for the full terms.

**Never negotiate under pressure.** If they ask for a discount and they're not a founding customer, the response is: "The annual plan is the best deal I can do. Happy to set that up if you want to go that route. Otherwise the monthly rate stands."
