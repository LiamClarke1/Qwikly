# Discovery Checklist — Qwikly Sales

---

## BEFORE THE CALL — Research (3 minutes)

Do this before every call, no exceptions.

### Their website
- [ ] Does it load quickly on mobile? (open on your phone, count to 3)
- [ ] Is there any existing chat widget, live chat, or inquiry form?
- [ ] Is there a visible phone number or WhatsApp button?
- [ ] Does it look like it was last updated recently, or is it dated?
- [ ] What's the main call to action? ("Call us", "Book online", "Contact us"?)

### Their Google Maps listing
- [ ] Are their business hours listed? Do they have after-hours?
- [ ] How many reviews do they have? Are they responding to them?
- [ ] What suburb/area are they based in?
- [ ] Are there competitor listings nearby with more reviews?

### Their Instagram or Facebook (if applicable)
- [ ] Are they active? When was the last post?
- [ ] Do they reply to comments or DMs?
- [ ] Are they running paid ads? (check for "Sponsored" posts)

## GOOGLE SHEET SETUP

If you haven't set it up yet, create a new Google Sheet called **Qwikly Prospects** with those exact column headers. Share it with yourself on mobile so you can log on the go. No CRM needed until you're past 50 prospects.

One row per business. One row per conversation if you speak to the same business twice (so you can see the history at a glance).
