# Founding 10 Customer Offer — Qwikly

---

## The Offer

The first 10 businesses to sign up to Qwikly Pro get **50% off for 6 months** (R299/month instead of R599/month) in exchange for:

1. A short written testimonial (2-4 sentences) that we can publish on the website
2. Permission to display their logo on the qwikly.co.za customers page

After 6 months, the plan rolls over to the standard Pro rate of R599/month. No surprises.

---

## Why This Works

- Founding customers get a real price break before anyone else
- You get social proof (logos + testimonials) to anchor the site before self-serve traffic arrives
- The exchange is fair and transparent, no hidden conditions
- "Founding 10" creates urgency without fake scarcity — it's a real limit

---

## How to Pitch It On a Call

Use this when you've got someone warm and they're close to committing:

> "We're opening spots for our first 10 customers and we're doing something a bit different for them. You'd get Qwikly Pro at half price for the first six months, so R299 a month instead of R599. The only thing we'd ask in return is a sentence or two about your experience once you've been using it, and permission to put your logo on our website. After six months it rolls to the regular rate. Interested in being one of the first ten?"

Then stop talking. Let them decide.
